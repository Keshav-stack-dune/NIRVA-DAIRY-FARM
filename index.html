Our Story

A Family's Promise of Purity

Nirva Dairy Farm · Est. 2025· Nagaur, Rajasthan

What began as a small family farm with 5 cows has grown into Nagaur's most trusted dairy. We started with one simple promise: to give every family access to the same quality of milk our own children drink.

Every animal on our farm is raised with love, fed natural grass, and never given artificial hormones. That's why our milk tastes different — because it truly is different.
