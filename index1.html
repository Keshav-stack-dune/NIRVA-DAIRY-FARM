Meet the Founder

From Farm Roots to Pure Nutrition

Kesharam Jandu
Founder, Nirva Dairy Farm · Nagaur, Rajasthan

What started as a small family farm with just 5 cows has today become a symbol of trust in Nagaur. Kesharam Jandu built Nirva Dairy with one simple belief — "The milk we serve should be as pure as the milk we give to our own family."

Every animal is cared for with love, fed naturally, and raised without artificial hormones. This isn't just dairy — it's a commitment to purity, health, and honesty.

💬 "Purity is not a claim, it's our daily promise."
